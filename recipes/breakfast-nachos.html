Breakfast Nachos

It's like a breakfast burrito but nachos

It's like nachos but with some breakfast things. Put whatever you want on it. Some avacado, salsa, sour cream, etc. Because the oven is really only to melt the cheese and finish up the dish, cook all the meat before putting it on the nachos. I also like to add a smaller layer of cheese directly on the chips to make everything adhere better. If you have some cheese dip you can drizzle it on top at the end.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • bacon
  • sausage
  • it's nachos add whatever

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. line baking sheet with foil
  3. arrange chips on foil
  4. cook chopped bacon and ground sausage
  5. scramble eggs in same pan
  6. spread scramble evenly across chips
  7. add cheese to your hearts desire
  8. bake for like 10-15 min or until stuff is melted
