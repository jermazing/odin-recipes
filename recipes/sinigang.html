Sinigang

A traditional filipino tamarind-base soup

People like adding filipino eggplant but eggplant is gross. Sometimes I'll add okra at the end with the spinach. Can be eaten on it's own or with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet sinigang mix
  • 2lbs Pork shoulder
  • 1 Large white onion
  • 4 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 Daikon radish
  • 2lbs bag of spinach

Instructions

  1. Saute diced onions and tomatoes in instant pot
  2. Add cubed meat
  3. Add sliced daikon radish
  4. fill with water to cover
  5. add whole packet of sinigang mix
  6. add fish sauce to taste
  7. pressure cook on high for 35 min
  8. natural release
  9. add spinach and cover for 5 min
